- Frontend active-date counting treated any `date_status: true` post as active, even when the post was blocked (`3`), deleted (`4`), or warned (`6`).

## Fixes Applied
- Updated `/lesociety/latest/home/node/secret-time-next/utils/dateState.js`
  - `isActiveDate()` now requires:
    - `date_status` to be published/true
    - `status` not to be one of `3`, `4`, or `6`
- Updated `/lesociety/latest/home/node/secret-time-next-api/controllers/v1/date.js`
  - Default `getAllDates()` query now excludes `status: 4` in addition to `3` and `6`

## Expected Result
